# DocuAId
AI-driven bureaucracy Navigation Help

Highly qualified Foreign people which had to leave their homecountry 

## Requirements: 
- High quality Multilingual support.
- Anthropic API for Multimodal Input/Document Analysis and 
- Personally fitted suggestions and application track
- Identify and handle PII information.
- Keep the required tech literacy minimal.

## User stories:
- Woman "Ann Bee" is within the process of applying for her recognition of her job qualifications and social benefits. Whenever she receives a new letter from the Behörde, she can fotograph or scan the Document (all pages) and input it into the app. The app processes the document and executes multiple tasks: Translate the Document into the preferred language of the User, additionally a summary with easy to understand next steps is generated, e.g. "call here" or "write a mail to..", "fill Form ZYX and send via mail". In case of e-mail, a proposal text is generated in both german and the preferred language to copy and paste into your own e-mail client.
- Track finished, open and "waiting for" applications in a personal history.
- Provide Help and suggest the relevant offices for your City if they want a human to ask for help.
